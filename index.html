Hi I'm Sara!

I'm an animator, & digital artist that focuses on deep, story-rich scenes with a focus on the macabre and old VHS aesthetics. While my style is extremely flexible, I mostly focus on cartoony characters and how they can be formed and melded to feel as expressive as possible. My main inspirations for my work come from artists such as Gooseworx, Andrew Hussie, & Jamie Hewlett. I am also enamored by old analog media as a whole, and quiet horror, the type that sits with you after watching something. You can see my sense of style in the work I did for the pilot of my indie show, BREAKROOM, along with the portraits and small animations I did for V.HC.S.: Project Zomboid Riverside. I am constantly doing my best to innovate on what I've done before, and try new things in my work. From the way I do backgrounds, to my shading style, it is constantly evolving.
